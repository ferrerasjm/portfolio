HEALTH

PBSP endeavors to improve the health and well-being of communities through innovative programs, partnerships, and sustainable solutions.

Community Health Initiative

Empowering local health workers and providing essential medical services to underserved areas.

Nutrition Support Program

Addressing malnutrition through education, food supplementation, and community engagement.

Mobile Health Clinics

Bringing quality healthcare directly to remote and vulnerable populations.

Every Filipino has a right to accessible and quality education. PBSP works with its members, partners, and donors in improving quality education for learners.

EDUCATION

Classroom Construction

We work with donors and partners to provide schools with disaster-resilient, PWD-inclusive and gender-sensitive classrooms.

Learning Continuity and Ready for School

We provide schools and students with educational assistance that help them continue basic education.

ENVIRONMENT

We only have one planet. PBSP seeks to improve resiliency and contribute in conservation efforts to ensure that future generations will still have a home.

Reforestation

We help protect and restore watersheds through tree planting initiatives.

Solid Waste Management

We seek to empower members, partners and communities to reduce and recycle waste.

Water for the Waterless

We provide schools and communities access to safe and clean water for drinking, hygiene, and sanitation.

LIVELIHOOD

We promote inclusive business and improve access to jobs through skills upgrading and employment facilitation. The program also seeks to expand economic opportunities through engagement of the private sector and partner organizations

Hyperlocal Economies

We help expand economic opportunities for communities and MSMEs.

Inclusive Business

We help farmers develop high value crops and link them to value chains of companies for a more sustainable livelihood.

Upskilling and Workforce Development

We provide the unemployed and underserved sectors with skills training and employment opportunities.
